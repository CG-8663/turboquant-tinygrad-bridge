# Prince Canuma — MLX-Audio

## Summary

Text-to-speech (TTS), speech-to-text (STT), and speech-to-speech (STS) library built on Apple's MLX framework.

## Key Details

- Efficient speech synthesis on Apple Silicon (M series chips)
- Multiple model support
- Part of Prince Canuma's broader MLX ecosystem (alongside MLX-VLM, MLX-Embeddings)

## Significance for Bridge

Demonstrates MLX ecosystem breadth — not just text/vision but audio modalities. Relevant for multi-modal inference pipelines that may eventually use the bridge.
